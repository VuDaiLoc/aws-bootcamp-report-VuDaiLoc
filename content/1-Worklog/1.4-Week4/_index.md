### Week 4 Objectives:

* Understand advanced AWS networking techniques: VPC Peering, Transit Gateway, VPC Endpoint, Direct Connect.
* Understand and deploy Elastic Load Balancing.

### Tasks to be carried out this week:

### Results achieved in week 4:

* Understood and successfully practiced VPC Peering between two VPCs.
* Grasped the hub-and-spoke architecture of AWS Transit Gateway and its use cases.
* Successfully configured VPC Endpoints (Gateway/Interface), accessing AWS services (S3, DynamoDB...) without going through the internet.
* Clearly distinguished Direct Connect (dedicated physical connection) from Site-to-Site VPN (encrypted internet connection).
* Successfully deployed an Application Load Balancer with a Target Group, verifying correct traffic distribution.
