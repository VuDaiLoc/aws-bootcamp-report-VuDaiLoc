# Summary Report: "Saturday Meetup"

### Event objectives

- Learn practical experiences and processes for participating in hackathon competitions from speakers' sharing
- Understand how to build and develop a real-world application from idea to finished product
- Update on the qualities, mindset, and soft skills needed to succeed in the technology field
- Build a foundation of knowledge and skills to apply to academic projects and personal projects

### Key highlights

- A group of students from FPT University shared their experiences participating in Hackathon competitions, introducing the process of ideation, teamwork, product development, and lessons learned during the competition.
- A speaker introduced the Đại Việt Tử Vi startup, sharing about the product concept, system architecture, development process, as well as the challenges and experiences when deploying a real-world application.

### Knowledge and Experience Gained

- Better understanding of the software product development process from idea to real-world deployment.
- Recognized the importance of Hackathon competitions in training teamwork skills, problem-solving, and developing professional expertise.
- Learned additional experience in system design and real-world application development from the Đại Việt Tử Vi startup sharing.

### Applying to Study and Work

- Apply the shared software development process to personal projects and academic projects.
- Reference the design and deployment approach of Đại Việt Tử Vi when building my own applications in the future.
- Continue to enhance knowledge of AWS, Cloud Computing, and software development to prepare for future projects and technology competitions.



### Event Experience

Attending the Saturday Meetup was a rewarding experience, giving me the opportunity to gain practical insights from speakers and FPT students who had participated in numerous projects and technology competitions.

The sharing about Hackathon helped me better understand the product development process, teamwork, and how to solve issues that arise during project development. Additionally, the introduction of the Đại Việt Tử Vi startup provided a practical perspective on building and operating a complete web application.
