**On this page**, you will need to introduce your worklog. **How** did you complete it? How many weeks did you take to complete the program? **What** did you do in those weeks?

Typically, and as a standard, a worklog is carried out over about 3 months (throughout the internship period) with weekly contents as follows:

**Week 1:** [Getting familiar with AWS and basic AWS services](1.1-week1/)

**Week 2:** [Account security (MFA, IAM Group) and cost management (AWS Budget)](1.2-week2/)

**Week 3:** [Amazon VPC, Subnet, Security Group and Site-to-Site VPN](1.3-week3/)

**Week 4:** [VPC Peering, Transit Gateway, VPC Endpoint and Elastic Load Balancing](1.4-week4/)

**Week 5:** [Amazon EC2: Instance Type, AMI, Key Pair and EBS](1.5-week5/)

**Week 6:** [Amazon S3 and AWS storage services](1.6-week6/)

**Week 7:** [Shared Responsibility Model, IAM, Cognito, Organizations and Identity Center](1.7-week7/)

**Week 8:** [AWS KMS (data encryption) and AWS Security Hub](1.8-week8/)

**Week 9:** [Building the specification and architecture for the AI-Powered Smart Document Assistant project](1.9-week9/)

**Week 10:** [Building the backend with Amplify Gen 2, Cognito Authentication and the data foundation](1.10-week10/)

**Week 11:** [Completing document management features (upload, view, download, delete) on S3/DynamoDB](1.11-week11/)

**Week 12:** [Building the AI pipeline: Amazon Textract (OCR) and Amazon Bedrock (summarization, classification)](1.12-week12/)
